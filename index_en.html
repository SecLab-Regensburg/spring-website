SPRING 12
07. - 08. June 2017

SIDAR's graduate workshop for reactive security (SPRING) is held in Frankfurt am Main at DE-CIX Meeting Center from 7th to the 8th of June 2017.

SPRING is organized by the special interest group Security - Intrusion Detection and Response (SIDAR) of the German Informatics Society (GI) and hosted by DE-CIX.

Participation is free of charge, but a binding registration is required.

 Location
DE-CIX Meeting Center, Frankfurt

The DE-CIX Meeting Center is located in the Frankfurt East End. It can be easily reached by public transport or by car. You will find further information about parking and how to get there on the DE-CIX Meeting Center website.

 Call for abstracts
Deadline: 21. April 2017

SPRING is a venue for presentation of early-stage research in the field of IT-Security. SPRING solicits submission of scientific papers presenting novel research on malware analysis, intrusion detection, and related systems security topics. As per our tradition, SPRING encourages submissions from the following broad areas:

  • Analysis of Vulnerabilities
  • Intrusion Detection
  • Malware
  • Incident Management
  • Forensics

Submission template download:  latex_template.tex (4950 bytes)

Please send your submission to:
spring2017 [at] de-cix (dot) net

Requirements

Please make sure that your contribution follows our guidelines for abstract submission:

  • English language
  • Not more than 2 pages of DIN A4
  • Usage of the provided template
  • PDF- and LaTeX-format
